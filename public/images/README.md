## Image Requirements

- **Hero Images**: High resolution (1920x800px or larger), landscape orientation
- **Detail Images**: High resolution (800x600px minimum), square or portrait orientation
- **Format**: JPEG or WebP
- **Background**: Hero images can have context backgrounds; detail images should have white/transparent or blurred backgrounds
- **Optimization**: Images will be automatically optimized by Next.js Image component

## SEO Alt Text

All images include descriptive alt text for SEO and accessibility:
- Hero images describe the scene and product context
- Detail images describe the specific feature being highlighted
